# Game Interface

Complete guide to Hytale's user interface.

## Main Screen (HUD)

The in-game interface displays essential information without obstructing your view.

### Health Bar

Located at the top left:
- **Hearts** - Your current health
- **Regeneration** - Slowly refills out of combat
- **Damage** - Hearts flash red

### Stamina Bar

Below the health bar:
- **Stamina** - Resource for sprint, dodge, special attacks
- **Regeneration** - Refills quickly when resting
- **Exhaustion** - Limited actions when empty

### Mana Bar

For magic users:
- **Mana** - Resource for spells
- **Regeneration** - Slow, accelerated by certain equipment
- **Spells** - Each spell consumes a variable amount

### Action Bar

At the bottom of the screen:
- **9 slots** - Quick access to items
- **Selection** - Keys 1-9 or scroll wheel
- **Indicator** - Active item highlighted

### Mini-map

At the top right:
- **Position** - Your location
- **Direction** - Built-in compass
- **POI** - Nearby points of interest
- **Players** - Allies in multiplayer

## Inventory

Press **E** to open the inventory.

### Sections

| Section | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Main bag | 40 storage slots |
| Equipment | Equipped armor and accessories |
| Quick crafting | Basic 2x2 crafting |
| Active effects | Current buffs and debuffs |

### Equipment

Equipment slots:
- **Head** - Helmets, hats
- **Torso** - Chestplates, robes
- **Legs** - Leggings, pants
- **Feet** - Boots, shoes
- **Main hand** - Weapons, tools
- **Off hand** - Shields, torches, grimoires
- **Accessories** - Rings, amulets (2-4 slots)

## Crafting Menu

Accessible via crafting stations:

### Workbench

- Basic and advanced recipes
- Category filters
- Search by name
- Saved favorites

### Forge

- Weapon and armor creation
- Equipment upgrades
- Item repair

### Kitchen

- Dish preparation
- Ingredient combinations
- Discovered recipes

## World Map

Press **M** for the full map.

### Features

- **Zoom** - Close or distant view
- **Markers** - Add your own points
- **Fog** - Unexplored areas hidden
- **Waypoints** - Guided navigation

### Marker Types

| Icon | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| House | Your base |
| Skull | Dangerous zone |
| Star | Point of interest |
| Exclamation mark | Active quest |
| Chest | Discovered loot |

## Quest Log

Press **J** for quests.

### Organization

- **Main quests** - Game story
- **Side quests** - Optional missions
- **Daily challenges** - Daily rewards
- **Achievements** - Permanent accomplishments

### Tracking

- Active quest displayed on screen
- Objectives with progress
- Previewed rewards

## Social Menus

### Friends List (O)

- Online/offline friends
- Group invitations
- Private messaging
- Custom status

### Chat (Enter)

- **Global** - All server players
- **Local** - Nearby players
- **Group** - Your team members
- **Whisper** - Private messages (/w name)
