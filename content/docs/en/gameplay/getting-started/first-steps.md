# First Steps in Hytale

Welcome to Orbis! This guide will accompany you through your first hours of gameplay.

## Launching the Game

### System Requirements

| Component | Minimum | Recommended |
|-----------|---------|-------------|
| OS | Windows 10 64-bit | Windows 11 64-bit |
| Processor | Intel i5-4460 / AMD FX-6300 | Intel i7-8700 / AMD Ryzen 5 3600 |
| RAM | 8 GB | 16 GB |
| GPU | GTX 960 / RX 560 | RTX 2060 / RX 5700 |
| Storage | 20 GB SSD | 50 GB SSD |

### First Launch

1. Download Hytale from [hytale.com](https://hytale.com)
2. Install the game
3. Log in with your Hytale account
4. Choose your game mode

## Character Creation

Before starting, customize your avatar:

- **Appearance** - Face, hairstyle, skin color
- **Clothing** - Starting outfit
- **Name** - Your identity on Orbis

## Your First Zone: Central Grasslands

You start in the **Central Grasslands**, the heart of Orbis.

### Initial Objectives

1. **Gather basic resources**
   - Wood (hit trees)
   - Stone (mine rocks)
   - Plant fibers (tall grass)

2. **Craft your first tools**
   - Wooden axe
   - Wooden pickaxe
   - Basic sword

3. **Build a shelter**
   - Find a safe location
   - Place walls and a roof
   - Add a door

4. **Survive the night**
   - Hostile creatures spawn at night
   - Stay inside or prepare for combat

## Tips for Getting Started

### Exploration

- Look for **points of interest** (POI) in the terrain
- Smoke columns indicate camps
- Ruined towers often hide treasures

### Combat

- Use **stamina** wisely
- Learn enemy attack patterns
- Block and dodge before counter-attacking

### Resources

- Gather everything you find at the beginning
- Chests in the world contain useful items
- Talk to NPCs for quests and tips

## Next Steps

Once you've mastered the basics:

1. Explore **dungeons** for better equipment
2. Learn **cooking** for buffs
3. Develop your **combat skills**
4. Discover new **regions**

:::tip Save
Hytale automatically saves your progress. In solo mode, you can also create manual saves.
:::
